Info

How to use this app:

  • Setup: Enter details in "Your Details" and save.
  • Add Shifts: Click a day. Select Duty Mode:
    • 1. Shift Only (Standard)
    • 2. Shift & On-Call (Adds call-out section)
    • 3. On-Call Only (Overrides shift, tracks call-outs)
  • Reports:
    • Timesheet Report: Sends standard shift hours (including Shift+OnCall hours) to "sthMriShiftData".
    • On-Call Report: Directly previews and sends duty details to "sthMriOncallData".
