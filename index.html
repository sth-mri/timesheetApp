How to use this app:

  • Setup: Enter your name, payroll number, email, and contracted hours in the "Your Details" section and click "Save Profile". This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Add Shifts: Click on any calendar day to add a shift. The app defaults to a standard 07:20 - 20:30 shift but allows you to adjust times, select Overtime/Bank Holiday types, and manage breaks or early/late deviations.
  • Reporting: At the end of the month, click "Generate Report". You can review your totals, print a PDF timesheet, or send the data directly to the central system using the "Send Shift Data" button.
